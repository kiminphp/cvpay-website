Compliance & Security

CVPAY is committed to operating a responsible payment infrastructure with a compliance-first mindset. We maintain internal controls designed to support KYC/AML requirements, risk monitoring, and secure operations.

Our Approach

  • Risk-based onboarding (KYC/KYB): procedures tailored to customer type and risk profile
  • Transaction monitoring: rules and alerts to identify anomalous patterns
  • Sanctions screening: applied where required and supported by our workflows
  • Ongoing reviews: periodic checks and enhanced due diligence when necessary
  • Recordkeeping & reporting: structured logs and documentation to support audits and investigations

Security Controls

  • Role-based access control and least-privilege policies
  • Encryption in transit and at rest where applicable
  • Secure key management practices
  • Infrastructure monitoring and incident response playbooks
  • Business continuity and disaster recovery planning

Transparency

We can provide compliance and security documentation under NDA, including policy summaries and operational procedures, subject to confidentiality and regulatory constraints.
